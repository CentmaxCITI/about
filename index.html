About CITI

Centmax Industrial Training Institute is an educational institution that is fully registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports under the Directorate of Industrial Training (DIT) and also a member of the Uganda Association of Private Vocational Institutions (UGAPRIVI), and the Business Technical Vocational Education and Training (BTVET). We have skilled clients from all over the world in skills such as GIS, Engineering Drawing and Design, Oil and Gas Engineering, Water Resource Management, Project Management, ICT and Urban planning.

Our E-learning atmosphere provides a lively, interactive and practical self paced learning with a student and tutor community of over 2000. You can therefore be assured of an enriching and immersive learning atmosphere with state-of-art visual aids that are recommended by Academia and Industry. We also have a thriving community of alumni across a range of sectors from Engineering, ICT, Environment, Business and Finance to name a few.
